Attack on Assam Rifles in Manipur

By Staff Reporter |22 Sept 2025

Aconvoy of Assam Rifles was attacked in Manipur . Insurgents opened fire, leavingseveral soldiers injured Security forces launched a search operation in the area. according to officials ,unidentified insurgents opened fire on convoy while it was patrolling. a highly sensitive area near Churachandpur district.

Security forces immediately retaliated , leading to a brief exchange of fire. Initial reports suggest that several soldiers sustained injuries while a massive search operation has been launched to track down the attackers.

The chief minister of Manipur has strongly condemned the attack and assured that those responsible will be brought to justice. He also expressed solidarity with the families of the brave soldiers.
